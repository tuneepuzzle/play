Tunee Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Tunee ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you visit our website (tuneepuzzle.github.io/play) ("Website") and play Tunee.

Information We Collect

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors use our Website and play Tunee. Google Analytics collects information such as:

  • IP address: Your computer's IP address is used to determine your general location.
  • Browser and device information: Information about the browser and device you are using to access our Website and play Tunee.
  • Website activity: Information about the pages you visit on our Website, how long you spend on each page, how you navigate through our Website, and your interactions with Tunee, such as game progress, scores, and in-game actions.

This information is collected in an anonymous and aggregated form and does not personally identify you.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected by Google Analytics to:

  • Understand how visitors use our Website and play Tunee.
  • Improve the user experience on our Website and in Tunee.
  • Track website traffic and popularity.
  • Analyze player behavior and game performance to improve Tunee.

Data Sharing

We do not share any personally identifiable information with third parties.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics is a web analytics service provided by Google that tracks and reports website traffic. You can learn more about Google's privacy policy here.

You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
